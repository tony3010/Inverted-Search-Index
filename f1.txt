Computer science is the study of algorithms, data structures, and problem solving techniques. Programming languages like C, Java, and Python help developers build software systems. Operating systems manage hardware resources and memory efficiently. Data structures such as arrays, linked lists, stacks, and queues are fundamental concepts. Learning programming improves logical thinking and debugging skills. Software engineering involves design, testing, and maintenance of applications. Databases store and retrieve information efficiently. Networking allows computers to communicate over the internet. Cybersecurity protects systems from attacks. Artificial intelligence and machine learning are modern advancements in computer science that transform industries worldwide.
